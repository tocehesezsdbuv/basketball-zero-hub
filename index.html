How it works

Sanctioned scrim rankings are built from the visible scoreboard stats across every logged scrim on the site. OVR now pulls from per-game production too, with scoring weighted the heaviest, then blocks, then assists, then ankle breaks, so the overall board reflects consistency instead of raw volume alone. The extra tabs compare points, blocks, assists, and ankle breaks on a game-by-game basis across the logged scrims for that region.
