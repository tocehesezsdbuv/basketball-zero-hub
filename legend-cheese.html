Monuments

Why He Matters

Previous Rival of Pocoyo

Cheese was one of the only players close enough to Pocoyo to make their rivalry feel like a battle between legends.

Ranked 1v2 Demon

Winning 1v2s in ranked separated Cheese from normal top players and showed rare control under pressure.

Impossible-to-Block Low Ping

His timing and releases were so fast that even correct reactions often felt late against him.

Pump Fake Gravity

Cheese's pump fakes were so convincing that defenders would bite almost instantly and give up easy openings.

Elite Offensive Threat

His offense was built on pressure, deception, and making defenders panic before he fully committed to a move.
